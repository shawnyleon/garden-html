Growing Beautiful Hibiscus

Congratulations on choosing to grow ornamental flowers! Hibiscus plants are a wonderful choice for adding vibrant colors to your urban garden.

Hibiscus Care Tips:

  • Place in a sunny location with at least 6 hours of direct sunlight
  • Water regularly, keeping soil moist but not waterlogged
  • Fertilize every 2-3 weeks during growing season
  • Prune in early spring to encourage bushy growth
  • Protect from cold temperatures below 50°F (10°C)

Hibiscus Tea Recipe

You can use the flowers from your hibiscus plant to make a delicious and refreshing tea:

  1. Collect 2-3 fresh hibiscus flowers
  2. Rinse the flowers gently
  3. Place in a teapot and add boiling water
  4. Steep for 5-7 minutes
  5. Add honey or sugar to taste
  6. Enjoy hot or cold!
